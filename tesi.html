PURPOSE
"Japan Matsuri, Borderless Experience" is a project that aims to enhance the experience of one of the most important Japanese festivals in Ticino, held annually in Bellinzona. Through visual engagement, the goal is to share with the audience the unique emotions and sensations that characterize the event, allowing them to relive that experience each time.
