JAPAN MATSURI

2/9

PURPOSE
Creation of a poster for the Japan Matsuri, the Japanese festival held annually at the Espocentro in Bellinzona, in Italian Switzerland.
DEVELOPMENT
The graphic style employs a simple language, emphasizing compositions through a palette of colors and geometric shapes, which constitute an indispensable element of the festival's identity. In Asian culture, geometric elements symbolize a sense of balance, order, and harmony.
