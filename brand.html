SPEECHLESS

8/8

PURPOSE
Creating a proto-logo based on the artist Pina Bausch involves designing a preliminary concept that captures the essence of her work. Pina Bausch was a renowned choreographer and dancer known for her innovative approach to dance theater.
DEVELOPMENT
The logo is named "SpeechLess." This combination of two words, "speech" and "less," was coined to generate an emphasized contrast, highlighting emotions, expressions, and individuality. "SpeechLess" aims to inspire people to overcome language barriers and express themselves boldly, without fear. This name embodies the power of taking risks and bringing deeper ideas to life.
