Work About me

OSCAR NEYMAR

4/8

PURPOSE
Development of a typographic font inspired by the architect Oscar Niemeyer.
DEVELOPMENT
The inspiration for our logo design originates from one of his quotes: 'Curves are the essence of my work.' As a result, we crafted a logo with an organic and light design, emphasizing curves to encapsulate his identity effectively. Central to both his persona and creations is the seemingly irrational order or the way in which his works may appear irrational but carry an underlying logic.
