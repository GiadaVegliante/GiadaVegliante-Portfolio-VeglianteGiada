THE KEY TO SONGS

8/9

PURPOSE
School project that relates music and moving images. The selected piece for this video is 'The Key to Songs' by Morton Subotnik from 1986.
DEVELOPMENT
The piece is interpreted through the representation of an artificial world inhabited by three-dimensional volumetric compositions. These compositions are influenced by physical forces that propel them to move, fall, break apart, and reassemble."
